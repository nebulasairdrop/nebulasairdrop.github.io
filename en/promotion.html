Nebulas Airdrop Promotion Program

Introduction

In order to promote the usage of Nebulas Airdrop and win the highest bonus of the Nebulas Incentive Program, we have conducted a complete promotion plan. In line with the incentive cycle of the Nebulas Incentive Program, we will set an airdrop on the site in the following week. Airdrop tokens are used to calculate the dividends. The funds come from the bonuses that the Nebulas dropped during the week in the Nebulas Incentive Program. At the same time, if we get the reward for the best application of the month, then the total amount of airdrop token in the four weeks of the month will be combined for additional dividends.

Rules

We award users who participating in the Nebulas Airdrop in a period at most 20% of bonus of the period.

Assume that the abbreviation for the airdrop token is NPT（NASPromotionToken）

  1. Each address can only gets once in an airdrop；
  2. Each address can get 100 NPT basically；
  3. You can get extra 40 NPT by filling in a valid referrer's address；
  4. Every time your friend successfully received an airdrop using your referrer's address, you can get extra 40NPT；
  5. After the end of one period, after the Nebulas Airdrop has received the official reward of the NAS (at 24:00 on Friday, the second week), we will automatically distribute the dividends in this period to all users participating in the airdrop within 2 days.

Dividend Distribution Algorithm

The dividend distribution algorithm in each bonus period is as follows:

Assume that during one period, the Nebulas Airdrop acquired N NAS coins in the Nebulas Incentive Program, the total supply of airdrop tokens is M, and the baseline is B=100000. The value of each token is:

NPT = 0.2 * N / (B+ M) NAS

The above formula has the following rules:

  1. In each period, as the total number of airdrop tokens increases, the proportion of bonuses used to reward users also increases. When the total number of airdrop tokens is equal to baseline B, the ratio is 10%. As the total number increases infinitely, the proportion becomes close to 20%. Users need to actively promote in order to obtain a larger proportion of rewards;
  2. The user may create a batch of addresses to get airdrops, but this action lowers the unit value of the NPT, and consider that each transaction needs to pay a $0.15-$0.3 fee, so when the value of token in a airdrop is less than transaction fees, the user will consider this action is a uneconomical behavior, and cheating cannot continue to exist;
  3. The spontaneous cheating airdrops and spreading by users may help the Nebulas Airdrop to win a higher reward, but the low balance accounts getting airdrops may not help the nebulas airdrop.

金主奖

介绍

为了宣传和推广星云空投应用，赢得星云激励计划的最高奖金，我们制定了金主奖。与星云激励计划的奖励周期一致，我们在本网站上每周设置一个金主奖，金主奖的代币做为权重用于计算本周"最豪金主"，奖励资金来源于星云空投该周的在星云激励计划中获得的奖金。同时如果获得月最佳应用的奖励，则以月作为周期选出本月"最豪金主"。

规则

我们每个奖励周期拿出奖金的10%设立金主奖，奖励本周期内"最豪金主"。

假设空投的代币的缩写为 GMP（GoldMasterPrize）

  1. 在一次金主奖竞选周期内每个地址只能领取一次；
  2. 基础空投的数量是每个地址1 GMP；
  3. 填写有效的推荐人的地址可以获得额外 0.0001 GMP；
  4. 每推荐一个朋友且成功领取空投后可以获得额外 0.0001 GMP；
  5. 一个周期结束后，当星云空投获得星云官方的奖励后（第二周周五24时），我们将在2天内自动把该周期内的"最豪金主"奖励发给该周期的"最豪金主"。

确定"最豪金主"的算法和补充规则

在每个金主奖竞选周期内的算法如下：

假设一个周期内有完整的N天(每天星云链产生5760个区块)，星云官方公布的本周期第一个区块高度为(H + 1)。以金主A1为例，假设金主A1有 G 个 GMP，Balance(A1, H)表示的是A1账号在区块高度为H时的NAS余额，金主A1的本周期总积分M为：

M = G * (Balance(A1, H + 5760) + Balance(A1, H + 5760 * 2) + ... + Balance(A1, H + 5760 * N)) / N

本周期的"最豪金主"即为通过上面公式计算的M值最大的账号

对算法的说明和补充：

  1. 每个周期内，每个账号GMP的值基本都约等于1，通过邀请朋友对GMP的值仅仅产生较小的影响，而M的值更取决于竞选金主们在本周期内的账户平均余额；
  2. 用户可能创建小号刷GMP的值，但是这种行为很容易被发现，如果发现账号有通过自动化脚本实现"不诚实"的邀请的行为则取消该账号的竞选资格；
  3. "最豪金主"每周期只竞选一名，M值最高的是本周期的"最豪金主"，不同周期"最豪金主"可以重复获奖。
